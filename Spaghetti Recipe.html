Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 jar (24 ounces) spaghetti sauce 2 cups water
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons McCormick® Basil Leaves
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons McCormick® Garlic Powder
  • 1 teaspoon McCormick® Oregano Leaves
  • 1/4 teaspoon McCormick® Crushed Red Pepper
  • Pepper 3 cups uncooked ziti
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese divided
  • 2 tablespoons grated Parmesan cheese

Instructions

  1. Brown ground beef in large skillet on medium-high heat. Drain fat
  2. Stir in spaghetti sauce, water, and seasonings. Bring to boil. Add pasta; mix well. Reduce heat to low; cover and simmer 20 minutes or until pasta is tender, stirring occasionally.
  3. Stir in 1 cup of the mozzarella cheese and Parmesan cheese. Sprinkle with remaining 1 cup mozzarella cheese. Cover. Let stand 5 minutes or until cheese is melted.
  4. Enjoy!
